About ANCT - TL

The National Alliance for Tobacco Control in Timor-Leste (ANCT-TL) is a coalition of organizations and individuals committed to reducing tobacco use and its harmful effects in our nation. Through education, advocacy, and community engagement, we work towards a healthier future for all Timorese.
